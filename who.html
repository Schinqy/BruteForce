Who we are

...The Story

Every company has its "Cinderella" story about those dark times when it has just started hitting the market. Our BruteForce story takes its beginning in 2017, at a small office with few like - minded geeks. Stemming from our mother startup company, Netro Electronics Zimbabwe, we envisioned the need for us to tap into the growing need of locally developed technoligical solutions. We strived to build a brand that will contribute to the world with useful products that empower people and make their lives easier. We still believe in it today.

...The Vision

BruteForce Engineering is a research and development company focused on the design and implementation of customized smart devices for domestic and industrial use. The scope of projects spans Robotics, Internet of Things (IoT), Automation, and Electronics. The company was founded with a main vision of fostering research, design and development of industrial technological products and services which is tailor made for our local environment. We spanned to offering technical and engineering services such as 3D Printing, Egg Incubators, Automation etc.
With our young and vibrant minds together with partners, you are guaranteed that your problem will get a well polished technical solution.
